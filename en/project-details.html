Project Description

The Global Business Tower is one of the most prominent projects implemented by Al-Mabani Contracting Company in the heart of Jeddah city. This tower is an architectural masterpiece that combines modernity and unique design. The tower consists of 35 floors and includes luxurious office spaces, commercial shops, and upscale restaurants.

The project was implemented according to the highest standards of quality and safety, using the latest construction technologies and high-quality materials to ensure the durability and longevity of the building. The tower is also equipped with the latest smart safety and energy systems to provide an ideal working environment for tenants.

Project Features

  • Unique architectural design that follows the latest global trends
  • Smart energy system that reduces electricity consumption
  • Advanced security and firefighting systems
  • Green spaces and modern interior designs
  • Multi-level parking spaces
  • 24/7 maintenance services
