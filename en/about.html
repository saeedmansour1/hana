Who We Are

A leading company in general contracting and construction in Saudi Arabia

Huna Arabia General Contracting Company

Huna Arabia General Contracting Company was established in 1995 and is one of the leading companies in construction and contracting in Saudi Arabia. For more than 25 years, we have been a trusted partner in executing many major projects throughout the Kingdom.

We provide integrated solutions in construction, starting from design and engineering consultations, to project execution and final delivery, while adhering to the highest standards of quality, safety, and professional efficiency.

Huna Arabia has a team of highly qualified engineers and experts, along with the latest equipment and technologies in construction, enabling us to execute projects with high efficiency and quality, while fully adhering to agreed delivery schedules.

Our Vision & Mission

The goals and principles we adhere to for excellence in contracting

Our Vision

To be the leading company in general contracting and construction in Saudi Arabia and the region, by providing innovative, high-quality solutions that meet our clients' aspirations and contribute to community development.

Our Mission

To provide integrated solutions in construction and general contracting, with a focus on quality, efficiency, and innovation, while adhering to the highest standards of safety and social responsibility, to achieve customer satisfaction and contribute to urban and economic development.

Our Values

The core principles that govern our work and relationships with clients and partners

Quality

We adhere to the highest quality standards in all our projects and services, using the best materials and latest technologies to ensure the durability and longevity of structures.

Commitment to Deadlines

We ensure adherence to project delivery schedules as planned, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Innovation

We constantly seek to adopt the latest technologies and innovations in construction to ensure we provide advanced solutions that meet our clients' needs.

Integrity

We maintain transparency and integrity in all our dealings with clients and partners, while maintaining complete confidentiality of project information.

Teamwork

We believe in the importance of teamwork and collaboration among all team members to achieve the best results and successfully complete projects.

Safety

We prioritize safety at all our work sites, applying strict safety standards to protect workers and property.
